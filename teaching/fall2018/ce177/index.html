Advanced Operating Systems

CE177 - Advanced Operating Systems

Semester: Fall 2018
Instructor: Hamid Fadishei
Location: Room 102, Engineering Complex 1

About the cours

CE177 is about advanced topics in operating systems design and principles. Topics such as kernel architectures, virtualization, distributed filesystems, energy efficiency, etc are covered throughout the semester. We will try to look at both established concepts and cutting edge researches. Students are actively involved by paper reading tasks.

Assignments

Please refer to the course lecture notes where assignments are embedded inline.

Lecture notes

  1. Vritualization
  2. Introduction to Distributed systems
  3. Communications in Distributed systems (We use A. Tanenbaum’s textbook slides1 for parts 2 and 3)
  4. Distributed Filesystems
  5. Energy Efficient Systems
  6. Kernel Architectures
